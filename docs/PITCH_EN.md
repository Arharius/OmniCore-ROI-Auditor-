## THE PROBLEM

Companies don't fail to automate because they don't want to. They fail because they can't prove — in numbers — that it's worth it.

The CFO's question is always the same: **what does this give us in money, and when does it pay back?**

The standard answer: an Excel sheet. Hours × rate = "savings". The CFO looks at it and says: "That's just an assumption."

Because it is.

---

## THE SOLUTION

**OmniCore ROI Auditor** — an audit tool that calculates ROI not by intuition, but by mathematics.

Three independent models. One number. One document.

**What it calculates:**
- Process graph (NetworkX) → finds the exact bottleneck where the company is losing money right now
- Absorbing Markov chain → computes mathematically expected deal time and conversion uplift
- Bayesian update → quantifies forecast confidence with 80% credible intervals
- 4-metric ROI → labor + errors + speed + conversion in EUR/year

**Output in under 5 minutes:**
CSV of client data → full ROI Passport PDF → ready to hand over immediately after the meeting.
